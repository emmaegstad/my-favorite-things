Nicolas Cage: Good or Bad?

Since the dawn of his career, we have been trying to answer the question: Nicolas Cage - good or bad? His iconic roles are polarizing, giving us horror masterpieces like Mandy, but also... Ghost Rider 2. In the words of Dan Harmon, "He's not good or bad. He's Nicolas Cage. There are lots of actors with whom we get bored and discard, if there wasn't something for sale with Cage he would have been gone long ago. He's a riveting performer, you can't ignore him." He may not fall squarely into either camp, but he is, without a doubt, my favorite.

Why We ❤️ Him

  • His freak-outs are legendary
  • Vampire's Kiss is a perfect movie
  • He has a encyclopedic knowledge of his craft
  • He both takes himself very seriously and is in on the joke
  • He once bought a haunted mansion so he could write a horror novel in it
  • He's the best
